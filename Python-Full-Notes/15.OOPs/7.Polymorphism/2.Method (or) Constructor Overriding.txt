		=======================================
				Method (or) Constructor Overriding
		=======================================
=>Method Overriding= Method Heading is Same + Method Body is Different
=>Constructor Overriding= Constructor Heading is Same + Constructor Body is Different
					(OR)
=>The Process of re-defining / overriding the  Original method of base class in various derived classes is called Method / Constructor  Overriding

=>To use Method / Constructor Overriding , we must apply Inheritance.
